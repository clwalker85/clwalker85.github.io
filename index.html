Coming soon, our online store will help expand our reach and streamline the process between purchase and shipment. We aspire to provide our suppliers with a significant competitive edge and save our customers time and money.

This begins with simplifying how our product is ordered. Virtually any device that can access the site can easily search for the seafood our customers demand. Checkout will require minimal payment and shipping information to be entered, and returning customers will find this information saved and password protected.

After checkout, much of the processing will be handled by a third-party bank, eliminating the risk of maintaining sensitive credit card data. After funds are cleared, the bank triggers the order immediately.

Once the order is submitted, our warehouses will be equipped to automate as much of the shipping process as possible. Maintaining quality and freshness is crucial to our business, so we aim to innovate and exceed expectations in this area.

The possibility for further growth also exists in leveraging analytics to guide customers to the products they want, to identify and reduce hidden costs, and to improve the experience of using the online store.
